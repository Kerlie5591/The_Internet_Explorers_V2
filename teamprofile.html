The advantage of knowing each group member’s learning style is also important, especially when collaborating over the internet. It allows the group to utilise techniques such as virtual whiteboards or transcripts of meetings to ensure each member can learn in the opportune way for them. Even if these psychometric tests are not 100% accurate for everyone, they provide a basis on which the group can utilise to work effectively, particularly at the start of a collaboration when individuals may not have experience with group work or specific team members.
